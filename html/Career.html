Future trends for placing in a job

Students of the department participate in projects that are implemented at the ITAS department laboratories. They take part in building automation systems and web-site development. Students also partake in development of automated intruder and fire alarm system, of video-surveillance and electrical billing systems, as well as workstations for an operator.

Together with engineers and teachers of the department students develop program products for commercial use, they also get practical experience in software and hardware complexes development. Undergraduates study modern methods and approaches of system-building, new technologies of object automation and system development.

Probation in VIENNA TU

In accordance with the Vienna Technical University agreement Perm STU students have an opportunity to work on probation in the under-mentioned institutes of:

  • Computer technologies (www.ict.tuwien.ac.at),
  • Information technologies (www.infosys.tuwien.ac.at),

There they execute degree works and term papers in different fields of VTU and PSTU interaction. Student's training and trips are paid at the expense of the department.

The ITAS department graduates are the most required specialists in the labour market. 99 % of students graduated from the ITAS department work in a chosen direction. In 2005 160 specialists on ASC (Automated system control) direction and 30 specialists on ECE (Electronics and computer engineering) direction graduated from the ITAS department. Labour market demand in Perm is about 300 specialists.

Authorized Courses

In the process of education students will also attend authorized courses on the following direction: «Administration of Oracle database», «Programming of Oracle database», «Real-time applications», «Construction technology of intellectual buildings» (see section laboratories).

A specialist graduated from the ITAS department has got knowledge in the following areas:

  • Designing of industrial networks;
  • Administration and designing of database (in Oracle, MySQL);
  • Development of automated software and hardware complexes;
  • Construction of intellectual building system (modern technologies EIB, LonWorks);
  • Web-programming;
  • «Special» system programming (automated system control of production and process);
  • Administration of operating systems;
  • Guarding in networks and safety of data system;
  • Network programming and administration;
  • Designing and development of soft hardware for compound object and distributed system control;
  • Development of video-surveillance system.

Theoretical studies will be exercised by students during practical work in ITAS department laboratories. The laboratories have got special equipment and software support that help students to reinforce the skills in development of information and automated systems.
