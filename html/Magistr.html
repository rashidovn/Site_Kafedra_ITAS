Degree (qualification) of a graduate – a Master of Science in Engineering and Technologies.

The «Information-calculating techniques» undergraduate course normally takes 6 years of full-time study. The basic educational programme of a Master of Science consists of a training programme of a Bachelor of Science on corresponding direction (4 years) and a specialized training of a Master of Science (2 years).

Professional skill description of a graduate

After graduating from the university a Master of Science in direction «Information-calculating techniques» can take up the posts of :

  • an engineer;
  • a programmer- engineer (a programmer);
  • an electronic- engineer (an electronic);
  • an automated control systems engineer;
  • a setup engineer and testing engineer

as well as other posts in accordance with his professional skill.

Professional occupation field

Informatics and computer engineering – is a brunch of science and technology that combines ways and methods of man's activity aimed at development and use of:

  • Computers, systems and networks;
  • automated system for data-processing and control;
  • automated design systems;
  • software support for computer techniques and automated systems.

Professional occupation objects

Professional occupation objects for a Master of Science in direction «Information-calculating techniques» are:

  • Computers, complexes, systems and networks;
  • automated system for data-processing and control;
  • automated design systems;
  • software support for computer techniques and automated systems (programs and software systems);
  • mathematical, informational, technical, ergonomic, organizational and legal support for the above-listed systems.

Kinds of professional occupation

After six years of university standard and specialized training a Master of Science in direction «Information-calculating techniques» can do the following kinds of activities:

  • development effort;
  • industrial and technical activities;
  • research work;
  • management activities;
  • operating activity;
  • pedagogical activity.

Specific kinds of activities are determined by educational and training programme, worked out by the university.
