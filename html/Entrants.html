The description of specialties.

The ITAS department prepares engineers - system-analyst in area of development of the automated control systems. In new academic year department declares students reception on the following specialties:
  • 220100 – «Computers, complexes, systems and networks» (specialty of ECT)
  • 220200 – «The Automated systems of information processing and management»(specialty of ACS)
  • 552800 – «Information technics» (bachelor degree of ICT)
  • 552822 – «The Distributed automated systems» (magistracy)

Experts graduated by our department works in all spheres where information technologies are applied, on various posts from the engineer-programmer up to the general director of the large enterprise.

Directions of enterprises activity are: Oil-and-gas industry, Bank activity, designing of complex technical systems, etc.

At training on faculty ITAS the following directions are considered:

  • Administration in networks
  • Designing corporate databases (Oracle, Microsoft SQL Server)
  • Automation in the industry (LonWorks, ModBus, P-NET)
  • Intellectual buildings (EIB, BACNet)
  • Automation of the distributed objects
  • Production management
  • Operational systems (Microsoft, Suse Linux)
  • Protection of the information and restoration of the data
  • Web-programming
  • Modeling systems
  • An artificial intellect


At training on department ITAS all students have access to the Internet with an opportunity to take the information about modern development in the field of information technologies and the automated systems.

Students have an opportunity to choice a training direction by themselves.

The department of ITAS has specialized laboratories in which development of directions of training and specialized hardware-software maintenance is carried out.
