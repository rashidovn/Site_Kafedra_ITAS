Independent robots and systems

Faculty ITAS participates in the project Robotics Project Tinyphoon Institute of Computer Technologies Technical University of Vien (TU of Vien).

Current project is consecrated on creation of robots - football players. It was formed by group of engineers-researchers and students of Vien TU. Tinyphoon, it is an opportunity to make positive contribution to development of robots and an artificial intellect.

Requirements to participants:

  • Knowledge of mathematics (higher mathematics, discrete mathematics, logistics), physics (mechanics)
  • Skills in modeling dynamic systems
  • Knowledge of programming languages: C++ or C#
