The regional center of automation (RCA)

Our center was formed in February, 2000 and became the successor of the Russian - Austrian fieldbus technologies studying center. The Russian - Austrian fieldbus technologies studying center was formed in April, 1997 within the framework of the cooperation program between the TU of Vien and the PSTU. Our Center is a member of the International Organization of P-NET and KNX Users.

Directions of activity:

  • Education technology of work with fieldbus-systems for automation;
  • Construction thermal energy and electric power account systems, security and fire signal system;
  • Education of experts in the following areas:
    • Manufactures automation;
    • Buildings automation;
    • Construction of the distributed applications;
    • Access to the distributed control systems by Internet;
    • Construction of databases on the basis of DBMS server;
    • Real-time systems adaptation;
  • Construction of operative-technological communication systems;
  • Laboratories development in the area of PCS;

  • Distance education to modern automation aids;
  • Translation and the publication of the specialized literature and methodical materials;
