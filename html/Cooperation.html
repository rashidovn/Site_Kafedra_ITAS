Cooperation with the technical university of Vien 2004-2006

The Perm State Technical University and the Technical University of Vien have projects since 1997. During the period since 1997 for 2003 department ITAS and institute of computer technologies TU of Vien had been executed the following works:

  1. Translation of the book on technology LonWorks
  2. Translation of the book on technology EIB
  3. Scientific manuals of dissertators (6 dissertations)
  4. Manual of students for preparation of diplomas (20 degree works)
  5. Installation of stands on technology LonWorks and P - NET in Perm State Technical University
  6. System engineering with application of modern industrial standards of automation.
  7. Holding of lectures and seminars on modern technologies of automation at the enterprises.
  8. Participation in seminars FET-1999, FET-2001
  9. Development of the software for monitoring systems of automation, the analysis of the report of interaction of devices in a network and so forth.

Directions of cooperation:

  • Modern technologies of building automation (EIB , BACNet , LonWorks)
  • Energy savings and audit
  • Data transmission on a power line
  • Wireless data transmission - (Bluetooth, ZigBee)
  • Mathematical bases of construction of systems. Models of systems

Joint performance of projects on 2004 - 2005:

  • System of distance education. (http://fieldbus.perm.ru). The department plans the organization of the removed training fieldbus to technologies, using the equipment of the Vien technical university.
  • Preparation of materials for the joint publication under joint projects
  • Scientific manual of dissertators (1 dissertation)
  • Filing for researches (grandees and competitions) in the Russian Federation and the European countries.
  • Manual of students for preparation of diplomas (2-3 diplomas). Diploma thesis:
    • The diploma on a subject offered Technical University of Vien by the student from the Perm State Technical University with an opportunity of passage of practice in Vienna.
    • The diploma on a subject offered Perm State Technical University by the student from the Viennese Technical University with an opportunity of passage of practice in Perm.
    • The joint diploma of students of the Technical University of Vien and the Perm State Technical University without departure other city
